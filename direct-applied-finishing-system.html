Direct Applied Finishing System (DAFS)

The system is applied over approved substrates in residential and low-rise commercial applications when exterior insulation is not required.

Acceptable DAFS Substrates:

  • Masonry Block/Brick
  • Fiberglass-mehs-reinforced cement board sheathing
  • DensGlass board sheathing (Just in ceilings)
  • Poured/Pre-cast concrete
  • New/Existing cement Stucco
  • Galvanized wire lath

Prior to Installation:

  • All surfaces must be thoroughly cured, dry and clean
  • The moisture content of existing substrates shall not exceed 16%
  • Minimum air and wall surface temperature of 50F or 10C must be maintained during and for 24 hours following installation

DAFS Installation:

  • Adhesives/Base Coats and fiberglass reinforcing mesh
  • Primer
  • Acrylic finish coat

The DAFS advantage:

  • Lightweight construction
  • Fade resistant color
  • Substantial savings on reduce structural requirements
  • Long lasting durability and easy maintenance
